We are assembling the top athletes from every class to compete to be the true champion of Illinois Track & Field.

Venue

We are excited to host this championship at the Hope Academy Track Stadium on a PROTRAXX™ Premier IAAF track certified by Connor Sports. This is an unrivaled surface and venue, making it prime conditions for your new PR.

Meet Info & Registration

Stay tuned for the schedule and registration information for the 2020 edition of the Illinois Meet of Champions.The meet will field championship competitions for every high school track event including:Long Jump, Pole Vault, High Jump, Shot Put, Triple Jump, Discus, 3200m, 100m High Hurdles, 100m, 800m, 400m, 300m Low Hurdles, 1600m, and 200m.Championship entries will be selected using the MileSplit Illinois Top Times list for each event.
There will be a limited number of small open competitions.
